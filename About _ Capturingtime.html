Capturing Time Through Photography

Freezing Fleeting Moments

I’m a Seattle-based photographer with a deep appreciation for the way a single frame can hold a fleeting moment,  a mix of light, emotion, and place that will never happen quite the same way again.
 
Photography has been part of my life since I was young, when I’d spend hours capturing nature and animals for local county fairs and my photography club. Over time, other parts of life took over, and my camera spent more time on the shelf than in my hands. But recently, street photography reignited that old spark. Watching others tell stories through everyday encounters reminded me why I fell in love with photography in the first place — for its ability to connect people, preserve moments, and reveal beauty in the ordinary.

This portfolio is a reflection of that renewed passion. It’s a space where I share some of my favorite photographs and the thoughts that came with creating them.

My Photography Mission

The Beauty of the Fleeting

My mission is to capture genuine moments that reflect real people and real stories. I want to preserve emotions and memories that might otherwise go unnoticed — the quiet interactions, the unguarded smiles, and the small details that make everyday life beautiful.
Whether it’s a planned portrait or a fleeting moment on the street, I approach each photograph with respect and curiosity. My goal is to honor the people and places I photograph, and to share images that feel honest, human, and alive.

I seek to capture the spirit of each passing moment, transforming it into something timeless — a visual echo of life’s beauty and the emotions that connect us all.
